Anecdote on surviving in Zimbabwe

Facing the task of providing for his siblings in Harare, Tinashe persevered by collecting discarded vegetables from the market. He cleaned and bundled them, selling them from a small cart. His determination made this humble effort into an amazing business, proving that trying hard can build a future from nothing.
